## WHAT YOU MUST PRODUCE

Design a **complete transformation system** with the following components. Be specific, not vague. Give actual schedules, actual techniques, actual resources. Treat the user as intelligent — do not over-explain basics, but do not assume habits exist that don't.

Explain the dopamine system state, the anhedonia indicators, the paradox of high GPA + total inertia, and why previous plans failed on Day 2. Reframe this not as laziness or character failure, but as a systems problem with a systems solution. Be honest. Do not sugarcoat.

### 2. THE CORE OPERATING PRINCIPLE
What is the single underlying design philosophy of this plan? (E.g., minimum effective dose, identity-based habits, deadline manufacturing, etc.) Justify why this philosophy fits this specific person rather than a generic user.

### 3. PHASE STRUCTURE
Break the ~24-week timeline into phases with clear goals per phase. Account for the fact that the first 2 weeks are the highest dropout risk. Design accordingly.
